About Covid-19

General Information

Covid-19 ,or the coronavirus,is a deadly pathogen originating from Wuhan, China.It is classified in a larger family of pathogens that include SARS and MERS which cause respiratory symptoms. It spreads at an alarming rate, and causes symptoms similar to the common cold or the flu.Severity of its symptoms can vary from being tame to extremely life threatening depending on the individuals circumstances. Covid-19 is extremely dangerous and contagious, if you or anyone you know suspects that they have come into contact with covid it is recommended that they contact a proper health care official to get tested.

Symptoms

The common symptoms of Covid-19 are as follows:

  • Fevers/chills
  • Coughing
  • Fatigue
  • Troubles breathing
  • Muscle/body aches
  • Loss of taste and smell

For more information on possible symptoms visit the CDC's symptom page.

Prevention

The three most common ways to prevent the spread are:

  • Wearing a mask

    • Wearing a mask is considered good practice during the pandemic, The cdc recommends that anyone over the age of two wears masks in crowded indoor/outdoor areas whether vaccinated or not.

  • Getting a Vaccine

    • Getting vaccinated is also a good option as well as it prevents the recipient from catching and spreading the virus.

  • Cleaning/Ventilating your Home

    • It is also considered good practice to air your home or living space out once in awhile as well as cleaning regularly to elminate the viruses chance of sticking around your home.
